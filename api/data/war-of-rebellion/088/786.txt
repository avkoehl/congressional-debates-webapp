WALTHALL STATION, September 11, 1864-12 m.

WALTHALL STATION, September 11, 1864-1.40 p. m.

Major FISHER,

Chief Signal Officer:

The following was intercepted:

12.45 p. m.

Colonel B[RENT]:

Two companies of infantry; first halted behind the cavalry in open space. They come from Chesterfield. Three wagons have also halted at same place.

R.,

Sergeant, at C.

H. W. HOLMAN,

Sergeant.

WALTHALL STATION, September 11, 1864-6 p. m.

Major FISHER,

Chief Signal Officer:

Colonel B[RENT]:

All quiet this p. m. No boats in sight on the river.

R.,

Sergeant.
