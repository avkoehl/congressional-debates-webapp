
GENERAL ORDERS, HDQRS. DISTRICT OF WEST FLORIDA, Numbers 29. Barrancas, March 27, 1865.

Surg. Thomas Howley, Twenty-fifth U. S. Colored Infantry, the senior surgeon in this command, is hereby announced as surgeon in charge of the District of West Florida, and will be obeyed and respected accordingly.

By order of Brigadier-General Asboth:

FRANK ENO,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
