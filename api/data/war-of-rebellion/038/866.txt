NEAR R. GIBSON'S, May 12, 1863-7 p. m.

Major MEMMINGER,

Assistant Adjutant-General, Vicksburg:

MAJOR: Your letter of this afternoon, with regard to the movements of my DIVISION, has just been received. In reply thereto, I have to state that my command moved at 6 o'clock.

Respectfully,

C. L. STEVENSON.
HDQRS. DEPT. MISS. AND E. La., Vicksburg, MISS., May 12, 1863.

Major General C. L. STEVENSON, near Vicksburg:

GENERAL: The lieutenant-general commanding directs that you follow the movement of General Loring and keep well up with him. The lieutenant-general commanding leaves direct for Bovina this evening.

I am, respectfully, your obedient servant,R. W. MEMMINGER,

Assistant Adjutant-General.

HDQRS. DEPT. OF Mississippi AND EASTERN LOUISIANA, Vicksburg, May 12, 1863.

Major General C. L. STEVENSON:

Send forward two of your brigades to the bridge, and stop the remainder of your DIVISION at Lanier's. The lieutenant-general will be in Bovina to-night.

J. THOMPSON,

Assistant Inspector-General.
