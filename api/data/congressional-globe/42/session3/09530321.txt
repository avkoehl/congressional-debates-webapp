Chap. CXLIV.—An Act relating to the Claim
of John B. Chapman.
Beit enacted by the Senate and House of Rep-
resentatives of the United States of America in
Congress assembled, That the Commissioner
of Indian Affairs be, and he is hereby, author-
ized and required to investigate the claim of
John B. Chapman against the Osage nation
of Indians, and report to Congress what, if
anything, is justly due said claimant from the
Indians or the Government; if anything, how
much.
