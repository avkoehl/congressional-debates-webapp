October 15.-Crossed Bull Run early, but returned to re-enforce General Buford. Moved to near Fairfax Station same night and t Wolf Run Shoals.

October 16.-Picketed Occoquan River until the 18th.

October 18.-Marched to Fairfax Station.

October 19.-Marched to Centreville.

October 20.-Marched to Bull Run.

October 21.-Marched to Warrenton.

October 22.-Marched to near Sulphur Springs.

Respectfully submitted.

J. P. TAYLOR,

Colonel, Commanding Brigade.

Captain H. W. WEIR,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
