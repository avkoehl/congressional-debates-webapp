COIN CONTRACTS.
Mr. ORTH presented thefollowing joint res-
olution of the General Assembly of the State of
Indiana; which was referred to the Committee
of Ways and Means when appointed :
Be it resolved, Sc., That our Senators in Congress
be instructed and our Representatives requested to
oppose by their influence and votes the passage of
any bill that shall specially legalize coin contracts
until the United States shall redeem its Treasury
notes in coin, and to oppose the enactment of any
law which shall have the effect to reduce the present
volume of paper money in use among the people of
the United States.
