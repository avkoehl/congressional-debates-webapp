REMARKS:

Lieutenant-Colonel Jones:

Transportation in part sent to the rear. Lost 1 Napoleon, disabled, Gettysburg. [Where is it?] Captured gotten. [Sic.] Needs men in Garber's and Green's companies, and 14 horses.

Colonel Brown:

Horses-many lame, though serviceable. Transportation insufficient. Lost 1 caisson, blown up at Gettysburg, 4 forges, 1 battery wagon, and a number of wagons captured on road. Needs men in all companies, excepting Hupp's wagons, and clothing.

Lieutenant-Colonel Nelson:

Transportation insufficient. Lost 6 forges and a number of wagons on road, captured. Needs forges and transportation; also clothing.

The [Second] Corps:

In all, 81 guns.

Major McIntosh:

Transportation sufficient when rear wagons return. Two of the forges loaned in battalion. Lost 1 gun, disabled and turned in, and 1 rear part of caisson. Needs men in Lusk's and Hurt's companies.

Major Poague:

Transportation insufficient. Forges borrowed from other battalion. Lost 1 caisson on march to Falling Waters. Needs men for Wyatt's and Brooke's companies, and 25 horses.

Major Pegram:

Transportation sufficient. Lost 1 10-pounder Parrott, disabled in action and sent to rear; 1 Napoleon, disabled and captured on road with General Imboden; 1 caisson, turned in; 1 caisson and 8 horses captured; 1 caisson disabled and left on the field; 3 caissons blown up by enemy, and 1 abandoned on march to Falling Waters. Needs detailed men to be returned to companies, and some conscripts for each.

Lieutenant-Colonel Garnett:

Transportation insufficient. Lost 2 guns, captured near Falling Waters; 2 guns turned over by Major Richardson to Captain Hurt, to save them; 6 caissons and 3 forges, captured under General Imboden; 70 horses and 40 sets of barness, captured or abandoned, besides ordnance stores. Needs no men, but general refitting.

Lieutenant-Colonel Cutts:

Caissons full. Transportation sufficient. The forge captured under General Imboden. Three of the 3-inch guns are navy Parrotts.

In all [Third Corps]:

In all, 77 guns.

Grand total:

In all, 241 guns.
