HEADQUARTERS CAVALRY CORPS, December 8, 1863.

GENERAL: I have the honor to forward the reports of division commanders, showing the duties performed by the Cavalry Corps during the recent operations of the army. These reports are sufficiently full without any additional report from these headquarters.

I have the honor to be, your obedient servant,A. PLEASONTON.

Brigadier General S. WILLIAMS, Adjt. General, Army of the Potomac.
