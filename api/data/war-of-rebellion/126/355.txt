GENERAL ORDERS, QUARTERMASTER-GENERAL'S OFFICE, Numbers 56. Washington, D. C., September 28, 1865.

The following order by the President of the United States, in relation to the reliquishment of the Government's control over all railroads in the State of Tennessee and their continuations in adjoining States, now occupied by the U. S. military authorities and no longer needed for military purposes, is published for the information of all officers and agents of the Quartermaster's Department.

M. C. MEIGS,

Brevet Major-General, U. S. Army, Quartermaster-General.
