
DREWRY'S BLUFF, June 3, 1864.

Major JOHN M. OTEY,

Assistant Adjutant-General:

Have just received the following report: "Three monitors, two gun-boats, and three transports lying below Howlett's."

Respectfully,

G. H. TERRETT,

Major, Commanding.
