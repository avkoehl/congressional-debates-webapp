HEADQUARTERS SIXTH ARMY CORPS,May 26, 1864-8.10 a.m.

Major-General HUMPHREYS:

As soon as the trains of the corps have crossed the North Anna, I propose withdrawing the troops from Warren's old entrenchments and placing [them] in the position on my right vacated by Russell. Is the bridge at Jericho Mills to be maintained, and does the major-general commanding desire me to guard it?

H. G. WRIGHT,

Major-General.
