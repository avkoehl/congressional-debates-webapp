SIMSPORT, May 19, 1864.

General LAWLER, Commanding Thirteenth Corps:

GENERAL: The major-general commanding directs me to inform you that the movement contemplated in his instructions of last night to the flank and rear of the enemy will not be made except in the event of an attack from him in front.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

GEO. B. DRAKE,

Assistant Adjutant-General.

(Same to Generals Smith and Emory.)
HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF THE GULF, Simsport, May 19, 1864.

Brigadier-General LAWLER,Commanding Detachment Thirteenth Corps:

GENERAL: You are directed by the major-general commanding to return, immediately upon the receipt of this order, to your camps near Simsport. You can return by the shortest line, or by the route you took from here, according as your judgment may designate.

Very respectfully, &amp;c.,

GEO. B. DRAKE,

Assistant Adjutant-General.

(Same to General Arnold.)

GENERAL ORDERS, HDQRS. DETACH, 13TH ARMY CORPS, No.-. Simsport, La., May 19, 1864.
