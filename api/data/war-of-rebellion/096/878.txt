HEADQUARTERS DISTRICT OF EASTERN VIRGINIA,Norfolk, Va., March 7, 1865.

Brigadier General GEORGE H. SHARPE,

Assistant Provost-Marshal-General, City Point, Va.:

Refugee who left Raleigh on Friday last reports that General Sherman was supposed to be at Cheraw, S. C., moving toward Fayetteville, N. C. It was also reported that General Johnston was in his front and intended giving him battle near Cheraw.

J. McENTEE,

Lieutenant-Colonel, &amp;c.
