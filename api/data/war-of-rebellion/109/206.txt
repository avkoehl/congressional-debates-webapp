HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF THE MISSOURI,Saint Louis, February 5k, 1862.

COMMANDING OFFICER AT CAIRO:

Send troops forward to General Grant as rapidly as possible. Fort Henry is being largely re-enforced from Bowling Green. The Fourteenth Iowa have left to-day, the Forty-third Illinois will go to-morrow, and the Second Iowa in a few days.

H. W. HALLECK,

Major-General, Commanding.

[7.]

LOUISVILLE, KY., February 6, 1862.

L. THOMAS,

Washington, D. C.:

I have directed the six Ohio and ten Indiana regiments to join the expedition up the Tennessee and Cumberland. Please place at my disposal such light batteries as are available.

D. C. BUELL.
