The proclamation of the Executive of the 3d instant authorized you "to call out and to cause to be mustered into the service of Virginia, from time to time, as the public exigencies may require, such additional number of volunteers as [you] may deem necessary. " My object in addressing you is respectfully to inquire whether you will authorize me to enlist a field battery of artillery, with the understanding that it is not to be ordered from the city of Richmond unless some special occasion of attack or defense, and then only to a convenient distance and for a brief period. In other words, I ask permission to raise a field battery on the was establishment for the special defense of the capital of the State.

I would respectfully suggest the following as the composition of the battery, to wit:
