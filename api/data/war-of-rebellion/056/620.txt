HEADQUARTERS CAVALRY BRIGADE, Near Beard's Bridge, November 1, 1863.

Major-General WHEELER,

Commanding Cavalry Corps:

GENERAL: In obedience to your orders I marched to Cleveland with my command this morning, and finding no instructions there, have camped near Beard's Bridge, 7 miles from town, with headquarters at the house of Mr. Surguine, that being the nearest place where forage can be obtained.
