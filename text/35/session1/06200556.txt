The resolution was reported, as follows:
Resolved, That the act of Hiram Paulding, a captain

Walker, was not authorized by the instructions which had
been given him from the Navy Department.
Resolved, That while wc have no reason to believe that
the said Paulding acted from any improper motives or in-
tention, yet we regard the ant m question as a grave error,
and deserving, for the reason already given, the disapproval
of the American Congress.
