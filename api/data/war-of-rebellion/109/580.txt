THURSDAY, AUGUST 4, 1864.

General GRANGER:

Any orders?

A. J. MYER,

Colonel.

6.10 a. M. - No.

GRANGER,

General.

ON BOARD THE LAURA.

Colonel A. J. MYER,

Dauphin Island:

7.30 a. M. - I just saw 150 rebel infantry land at Fort Gainess from Mobile.

DENICKE,

Lieutenant.

DAUPHIN ISLAND.

General GRANGER,

Laura:

Brigadier-General McGinnis is here.

A. J. MYER,

Colonel.

DAUPHIN ISLAND - 9 a. M.

Lieutenant DENICKE:

I am going up the shore now.

A. J. MYER,

Colonel.

ON BOARD LAURA.

Colonel MYER:

Some eighty rebel infantry were just landed at Fort Gaines from Fort Morgan.
