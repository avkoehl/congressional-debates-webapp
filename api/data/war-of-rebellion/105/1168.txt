HEADQUARTERS DISTRICT OF OREGON,Fort Vancouver, Wash. Ter., June 30, 1862.

W. H. RECTOR, Esq.,

Superintendent of Indian Affairs, Salem, Oreg.:

SIR: I have the honor to inform you that Major J. S. Rinearson, First Oregon Cavalry, with Company C of that regiment, has been this day ordered to proceed to and take post at Camp Baker, near Jacksonville, Oreg., in compliance with your request for a miitary force in that section.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

JUSTUS STEINBERGER,

Colonel First Washington Territory Infantry, Commanding District.

Abstract from return of the Department of the Pacific, Brigadier General George Wright, U. S. Army, commanding, for the month of June, 1862.
