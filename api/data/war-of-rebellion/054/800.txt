COLUMBUS, KY., October 31, 1863.

Brigadier-General REID:

A large force is approaching Paducah. Please ask Admiral Porter to send a gunboat there at once, if practicable.

A. J. SMITH,

Brigadier-General.
SPECIAL ORDERS, HDQRS. DEPT. AND ARMY OF THE Tennessee,Numbers 6. Waterloo, Ala., October 31, 1863.

I. Pursuant to instructions from the general commanding the Military Division of the Mississippi, the Third Division of the Fifteenth Army Corps, Brigadier-General Tuttle commanding, will move at once by boats, via Cairo and up the Tennessee River, landing opposite Eastport, and push forward, via Florence, to join its proper corps.

By order of Major General W. T. Sherman:

R. M. SAWYER,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
