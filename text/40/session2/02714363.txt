consider the bill (II. R. No. 1170) granting a
pension to Mary Cover, widow of Samuel Cover,

It directs the Secretary of the Interior to place
on the pension-roll, subject to the provisions
and limitations of the pension laws, the name

vate is entitled to under existing laws, to com-
mence from the 10th of February, 18G3,and to

years of age.

amendment, ordered to a third reading, read
