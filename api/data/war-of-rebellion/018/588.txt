HEADQUARTERS ARMY OF VIRGINIA,Camp near Cedar Mountain, August 17, 1862.

(Received 9.25 a. m.)

Major General H. W. HALLECK:

I need 1,500 cavalry horses as soon as they can be sent. Our cavalry is much broken down, their horses not having been fit for anything from the beginning. Will your order them sent immediately?

The proper papers will go forward.

JNO. POPE,

Major-General, Commanding.
