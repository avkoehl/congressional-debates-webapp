PADUCAH, KY., November 30, 1861.

ASSISTANT ADJUTANT-GENERAL:

Enemy, 600 to 1,000 strong, reported in camp at Caseyville, with intention to attack. I have sent three companies and a howitzer to Cave in Rock; also Conestoga, as circumstances may require.

C. F. SMITH,

Brigadier-General, Commanding.
