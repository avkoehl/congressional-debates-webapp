
The casualties in this command were: At the crossing of the Little Congaree, 5 enlisted men killed and 10 wounded; total, 15. At the crossing of Broad River, 1 commissioned officer and 4 enlisted men wounded; total, 5. Please see list of casualties annexed. I shall forward reports of brigade commanders as soon as they are received.

I have the honor, major, to be, very respectfully, your obedient servant,

CHAS. R. WOODS,

Brevet Major-General.

Major MAX WOODHULL,

Assistant Adjutant-General, Fifteenth Army Corps.
