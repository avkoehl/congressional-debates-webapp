
FEBRUARY 27 - MARCH 28, 1865. - Expedition from Winchester to the front of Petersburg, Va.

SUMMARY OF THE PRINCIPAL EVENTS.

Feb. 27, 1865. - Sheridan's command starts from Winchester.

March 1, 1865. - Skirmish at Mount Crawford.

2, 1865. - Occupation of Staunton.

Affair at Swoope's Depot.

Engagement at Waynesborough.

3, 1865. - Occupation of Charlottesville.

8, 1865. - Skirmish at Duguidsville.

9, 1865. - Occupation of Columbia.

11, 1865. - Skirmish at Goochland Court-House.

13, 1865. - Skirmish near Beaver Dam Station.

14, 1865. - Skirmish at the South Anna Bridge.

15, 1865. - Skirmish at Hanover Court-House.

Skirmish near Ashland.

26, 1865. - Sheridan's command crosses the James River.
