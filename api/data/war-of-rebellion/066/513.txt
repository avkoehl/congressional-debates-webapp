SPECIAL ORDERS, HDQRS. DEPT. OF S. C., GA., AND FLA., Numbers 144. Charleston, S. C., May 25, 1864.

I. Major General Lafayette McLaws, Provisional Army, C. S., having reported at these headquarters in pursuance of paragraph XXIII, Special Orders, 115, Adjutant and Inspector General's Office, current series, is assigned to duty in this department, and will proceed to Savannah, Ga., and assume command of the District of Georgia and the Third Military District of South Carolina, relieving Major General J. F. Gilmer.

On being relieved Major General J. F. Gilmer will comply with the instructions he has received from the War Department.

* * * * *

By command of Major-General Jones:

H. W. FEILDEN,Assistant Adjutant-General.
