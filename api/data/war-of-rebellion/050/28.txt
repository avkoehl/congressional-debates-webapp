19-20, 1863.-Battle of Chickamauga, Ga.

21, 1863.-Skirmishes at Rossville, Lookout Church,and Dry Valley, Ga.

22, 1863.-Skirmishes at Missionary Ridge and Shallow Ford Gap,near Chattanooga, Tenn.
