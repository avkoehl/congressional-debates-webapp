The following is a list of casualties near Morrisville, October 12: Major Josiah Hall and Private Norman A. Morris, Company F, missing.

At Buckland Mills, October 19, Sergt. Horace K. Ide, Company D, wounded in the shoulder; Private Dennis G. Leahy, Company C, wounded slightly, and Corpls. Henry J. Hicks and Alexander Blo, Company M, missing.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

EDWARD B. SAWYER,

Colonel, Commanding.
