horses, &c., were seized by order of General
Sheridan, appraised by three appraisers ap-
pointed by General Devin, taken to Harper's
Ferry, most of it then sent to Pennsylvania
and sold, and the inonev, the proceeds of the
sale, covered into the Treasury, where it has
ever since been lodged. This bill only pro-
vides for that class of claims.
