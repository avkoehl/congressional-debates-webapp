Major General J. E. B. STUART.

Numbers 299. Report of Colonel Bradley T. Johnson, First Maryland Infantry, commanding Maryland Line, of operations May 11.

HANOVER JUNCTION, May 11, 1864-10.15 a.m.

General Stuart engaged the enemy at Ashland this morning and drove them out with loss. Their column still moving on Richmond.

BRADLEY T. JOHNSON,

Colonel, Commanding.

Honorable J. A. SEDDON, Secretary of War.
