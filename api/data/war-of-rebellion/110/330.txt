TUPELO, MISS., July 22, 1862.

Presidnet JEFFERSON DAVIS,

Richmond, Va.:

Obstacles in front connected with danger to Chattanooga induce a change of base. Fully impressed with great importance of that line, am moving to East Tennessee. Produce rapid offencive from there following the consternation now being produced by our caavlary. Leve this State amply protected by Van Dorn at Vicxksburg and Price here.
