[Inclosure Numbers 1 to third indorsement.]
HDQRS. U. S. FORCES, FORTS HENRY, HEIMAN AND DONELSON,November 30, 1862.

Brigadier-General CURTIS, Saint Louis, Mo.

GENERAL: As I know have a considerable excess of rebel prisoners and have received authority to make exchanges I have the honor to request that the following men of my regiment, paroled and now on duty at Benton Barracks, be returned here as soon as possible to be exchanged: Sergts. James H. Wing, George McLeane; Corpl. George Loppin; Privates C. S. Franklin, O. H. Bowman, R. Braschinsky, P. A. Bevington, P. McGeary, George R. Travor, Company B., Iowa paroled prisoners, Barracks Numbers 61, Benton Barracks.

I am, sir, your obedient servant,

W. W. LOWE,

Colonel, Commanding.
