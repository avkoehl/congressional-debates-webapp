SAINT LOUIS, MO., June 30, 1863.

Brigadier-General DAVIDSON, Arcadia:

Have you any further news from Price or Marmaduke? They may attempt a raid into Southwest Missouri, via Salem, Arkansas, and Lebanon. Keep a lookout in that direction. I have report this morning that Price is between Arkansas and White Rivers, below Little Rock, but doubt its truth.

J. M. SCHOFIELD,

Major-General.
