III. So much of paragraph 10, Special Orders, Numbers 98, current series, from these headquarters, as includes the One hundred and eighty-second Ohio Volunteer Infantry in the organization, First Brigade, First Division, Department of the Cumberland, is hereby revoked, and the Forty-fifth Wisconsin Volunteers is substituted in its place, the former regiment being on duty with Brigadier-General Tower.

By command of Major-General Thomas:

SOUTHARD HOFFMAN,
