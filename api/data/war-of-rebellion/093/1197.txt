V. Lieutenant Colonel L. H. Carpenter, Fifth U. S. Colored Cavalry, in addition to his present duties, will assume command of Camp Nelson, reporting as commandant of the camp to Brigadier General N. C. McLean, commanding First Division, Military District of Kentucky.

J. BATES DICKSON,
