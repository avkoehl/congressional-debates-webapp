IN SENATE.
Friday, December 7, 1855.
The Journal of yesterday was read and approved.
NOTICES OF BILLS.
Mr. WELLER gave notice of his intention to
ask leave to introduce the following bills:
A bill to amend an act entitled " An act to
establish a United States circuit court in the State
of California," approved March 3, 1855.
A bill to authorize and facilitate the construc-
tion of a Pacific'railrond and magnetic telegraph
through the territories of the United States.
EXECUTIVE BUSINESS.
Several messages in writing were received from
the President of the United States, by Mr. Sib-
net Webster, his Secretary.
The PRESIDENT. The messages are of an
executive character.
Mr. WELLER. I move that the Senate pro-
ceed to the consideration of executive business.
The motion was agreed to; and after some
time spent in executive session, the doors were
reopened, and the Senate adjourned.
