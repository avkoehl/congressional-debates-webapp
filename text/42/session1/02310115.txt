A message from the Senate, by Mr. Symp-
son, one of its clerks, informed the House that
the Senate had passed bills of the following
titles, in which the concurrence of the House
was requested:
A bill (S. No. 137) to authorize the payment
of duplicate checks of distributing officers;
A bill (S. No. 53) for the restoration of
Commander George A. Stevens, United States
Navy, to the active from the retired list; and
A bill (S. No. 202) authorizing the Presi-
dent to withdraw his acceptance of the resig-
nation of R. H. Lamson, late lieutenant Uni-
ted States Navy,
The message also announced that the Senate
had passed the joint resolution (H. R. No. 29)
to authorize the commissioners to revise the
statutes to print their report, with amend-
ments, in which the concurrence of the House
was requested.
