HEADQUARTERS VALLEY DISTRICT,August 3, 1864.

General BRECKINRIDGE,

Commanding:

GENERAL: Lieutenant-General early directs that you send a brigade of infantry at once to Martinsburg, with instructions to picket the various roads. It is intended to protect a train of wagons which goes to-day to Hedgesville for leather, and you will direct it to remain in Martinsburg until after the return of the train to-night.

Respectfully, &amp;c.,

A. S. PENDLETON,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
