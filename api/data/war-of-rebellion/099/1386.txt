HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF NORTH CAROLINA,March 13, 1865-6. 15 p. m.

Major General D. H. HILL,

Commanding Lee's Corps:

The general commanding directs that you will continue your march, without unnecessary delay, to morrow morning, to Smithfield. Upon reaching there report your arrival to General J. E. Johnston, at Raleigh, by telegraph.

Very respectfully, general, your obedient servant,

JOHN B. SALE,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
