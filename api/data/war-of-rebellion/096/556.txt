
GENERAL ORDERS, HEADQUARTERS SECOND ARMY CORPS, Numbers 4. February 14, 1865.

In accordance with Special Orders, Numbers 18, headquarters Armies of the United States, dated City Point, Va., January 24, 1865, Lieutenant C. H. Campbell, First New York Artillery, is announced as acting aide-de-camp to the major-general commanding the corps. He will be obeyed and respected accordingly.

By order of Major-General Humphreys:

CHAS. A. WHITTIER,

Assistant Adjutant-General.

HEADQUARTERS FIFTH ARMY CORPS,

February 14, 1865-9 a. m.

Brevet Major-General WEBB,

Chief of Staff:

I have the honor to report that nothing of importance occurred in my front during the past night. A detail of 1,000 men is at work this morning preparing the abatis in front of the breast-works near Hatcher's Run.

Respectfully,

G. K. WARREN,

Major-General, Commanding.
