
NEW ORLEANS, May 25, 1864.

Major GEORGE B. DRAKE, A. A. G., Dept. of the Gulf:

SIR: I have the honor to submit the following as the best estimate of the cavalry forces in the Department of the Gulf which can be made up from the data now in this office.

I am, sir, very truly, your obedient servant,

W. BARCOCK,

Lieutenant Colonel Seventy-fifth N. Y. Vols., Acting Aide-de-Camp.
