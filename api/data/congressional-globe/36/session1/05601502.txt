court, shall only be allowed where the matter in

error or appeal shall be allowed to the Supreme
Court of the TJnited States from final judgments
or decrees hereafter rendered in civil actions, and
in suits in equity, and causes of admiralty and
maritime jurisdiction, in a circuit court, or dis-
trict court acting as a circuit court, unless the
