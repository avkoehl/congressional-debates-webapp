LEXINGTON, September 13, 1864.

Brigadier General J. D. WEBSTER,

Nashville, Tenn.:

With the approval of Major-General Halleck, I am about to move with my available cavalry force to co-operate, with General Gillem in an attack on the salt-works near Abingdon, Va. Reports reach me of a rebel force near Sparta. Is there any truth in them?

S. G. BURBRIDGE,

Brevet Major-General, Commanding.
