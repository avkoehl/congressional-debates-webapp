HEADQUARTERS,Saint Louis, December 31, 1862.

Major General H. W. HALLECK,

General-in-Chief.

Jeff. Thompson, overestimated, does not advance. New Madrid re-occupied. Will arrest officer who abandoned it against my orders. Will try to keep every points safe. General Blunt destroyed four rebel steamers and much property at Van Buren, bringing away all he could transport. He falls back for supplies.

SAML. R. CURTIS,

Major-General.

JEFFERSON CITY, MO., December 31, 1862.

His Excellency ABRAHAM LINCOLN,

President of the United States:

I have stopped all assessments of Enrolled Militia. Please order by telegraph the suspension of all assessments by United States officers. Great distress is produced.

H. R. GAMBLE,

Governor of Missouri.

Abstract from return of the Department of the Missouri, Major General Samuel R. Curtis commanding, for the month of December, 1862; headquarters Saint Louis, Mo.
