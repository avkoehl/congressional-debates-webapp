HEADQUARTERS DEFENSES OF NEW ORLEANS, New Orleans, February 6, 1865.

Colonel W. SAYLES, Bayou Goula, via Donaldsonville:

Brigadier-General Sherman directs me to inform you that you will continue the occupation of Bayou Goula until ordered away. A full and complete report on all the subjects in your instructions will be expected as soon as practicable to send it. Acknowledge receipt.

FREDERICK SPEED,Assistant Adjutant-General.
