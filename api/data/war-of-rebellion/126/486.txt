WAR DEPT., PROVOST-MARSHAL-GENERAL'S OFFICE,

Washington, D. C., November 8, 1865.

Honorable EDWIN. M. STANTON,

Secretary of War:

SIR: I have the honor to submit my annual report of the operations of the Bureau of the Provost-Marshal-General of the United States for the year ending November 1, 1865.

On the 1st of November, 1864, the date to which my last annual report was brought up, the business of recruiting and the draft under the call of July 18, 1864, was in progress.
