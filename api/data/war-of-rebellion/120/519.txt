WASHINGTON, D. C., August 1, 1864.

Major General B. F. BUTLER, U. S. Army, &c.:

SIR: It appears by letters dated at Libby Prison, Richmond, that Lieutenant Charles W. Pavey, Lieutenant Markbreit, Lieutenant Duchesney, Captain Driscoll, were placed in cell confinement, as is supposed for some alleged confinement of prisoners held in the North.
