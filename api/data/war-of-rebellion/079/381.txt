IN CAMP, NEAR VALLEY STORE, GA.,

October 20, 1864.

[General J. D. MORGAN:]

GENERAL: I am directed by Major-General Schofield to inform you that the command will march at 6 a. m. to-morrow on the road to Alpine, General Wagner's DIVISION in the advance, and your troops next; then the trains and cattle of both DIVISIONS. The general requests that you detail a regiment to move in rear of all as rear guard.

I am, general, very respectfully, your obedient servant,

WM. M. WHERRY,

Major and Aide-de-Camp.
