BRIDGE ACROSS MISSOURI RIVER.
Mr. BURDETT also introduced a bill (H.
R. No. 108) to authorize the construction of a
bridge across the Missouri river at Boonville,
Missouri, and declare the same a post route;
which was read a first and Becond time. .
Mr. BURDETT. I move that this bill be
referred to the Committee on the Post Office
and Post Roads.
Mr. FINKELNBURG. I would suggest that
this bill should be referred to the Committee
on Commerce.
Mr. BURDETT. There is a clause in it to
make it a post road.
Mr. FINKELNBURG. That makes no
difference.
Mr. BURDETT. I am not very particular
about it; but I think it should go to the Com-
mittee on the Post Office and Post Roads.
The motion to refer the bill to the Commit-
tee on the Post Office and Post Roads was
agreed to.
PUBLIC BUILDING AT JEFFERSON, MISSOURI.

Appropriations:
A joint resolution of the General Assembly of the

Mr. BURDETT. Very well; I will move
that reference.
