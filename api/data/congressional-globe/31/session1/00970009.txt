IN SENATE.
Friday, December 7, 1849.
The Senate was called to order at twelve o'clock.
The Journal was read, and, after an interval of
some time, there being no business before the body,
the Senate adjourned.
HOUSE OP REPRESENTATIVES.
Friday, December 7, 1849.
The House was called to order at 12 o'clock m.
Mr. HILLIARD rose and noticed an error which
had occurred on the part of the tellers yesterday.
The name of Mr. Holladay, had by them erro-
neously been placed in the list of those gentlemen
who voted for Mr. Winthrop. The Journal,
however, was properly made up. He made the
statement at the request of the Clerk.
