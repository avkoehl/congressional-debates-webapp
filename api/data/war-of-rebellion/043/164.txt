---------------

*During the interval betwen the death of General Reynolds and the arrival of General Hancock, on the afternoon of July 1, all the troops on the field of battle were commanded by General Howard, General Schurz taking command of the Eleventh Corps, And General Schimmelfennig of the Third Division.
