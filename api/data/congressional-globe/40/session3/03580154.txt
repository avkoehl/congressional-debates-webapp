reference to our postal service, embracing

sequence of robbery or burglary, are sent, to

Roads, to be passed upon by that committee.
Claims in connection with the revenue, grow-
ing out ot mistakes in the administration of
the revenue laws, are sent to the Committee

claims of different kinds.

mittee than in another.
