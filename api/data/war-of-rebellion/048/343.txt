HEADQUARTERS CAVALRY CORPS,October 17, 1863-9 a.m.

GENERAL: General Kilpatrick reports the streams toward Thoroughfare Gap so swollen his scouts could not cross. The enemy have their cavalry massed at Gainesville.

A. PLEASONTON.

Major-General.

Major-General HUMPHREYS.
HDQRS. CAVALRY CORPS, ARMY OF THE POTOMAC,November 3, 1863

GENERAL: Herewith I have the honor to forward complete nominal lists* and corrected numerical lists of the casualties in this corps form the time of leaving the Rapidan, in the late movement of the army, up to the 22nd ultimo.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

A. PLEASONTON

Major-General.

Brigadier General S. WILLIAMS,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
