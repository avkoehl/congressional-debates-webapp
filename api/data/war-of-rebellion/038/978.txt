Vicksburg, June 25, 1863.

GENERAL: The commanding general directs that you issue orders to your command that none thereof hold any conversation with the enemy across the line, unless specially directed by you for specific purposes.

R. W. MEMMINGER,

Assistant Adjutant-General.

(To Generals Bowen, Forney, Smith, and Stevenson.)

SIGNAL CORPS, SMITH'S DIVISION, Post, June 25, 1863 Major General MARTIN L. SMITH:

[Intercepted.]

Commodore PORKER:

The departure takes place at 3 p. m. Can I have your permission to remain till it is over? The mortars are bursting very high.

SELFRIDGE,

Captain.

MAX. T,. DAVIDSON.
