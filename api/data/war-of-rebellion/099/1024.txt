I have no news from the cavalry to-day; would like very much if arrangements could be made for me to get information from General Wheeler. I have established a relay of couriers to Branchville, but have only one man at each post as yet. I have sent the Blakely gun of Zimmerman's battery to Branchville, owing to scarcity of horses in his battery. Is this right? If you can procure a map of this county do get it for me.

GEO. P. HARRISON, Jr.,

Colonel, Commanding.
