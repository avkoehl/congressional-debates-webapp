GENERAL ORDERS, HDQRS. DISTRICT OF WEST TENN.,

Numbers 30. Savannah, March 31, 1862.

The headquarters of the District of West Tennessee is hereby changed to Pittsburg. An office will be continued at Savannah, where all official communications may be left by troops having easier access with that point than Pittsburg.

By command of Major-General Grant:

JNumbers A. RAWLINS,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
