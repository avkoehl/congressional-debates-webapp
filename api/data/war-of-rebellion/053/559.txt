
OKOLONA, August 27, 1863.

General CHALMERS:

General Jackson reports enemy, infantry and cavalry, moving toward Yazoo City from Vicksburg. Hold your command now at Grenada in readiness to join Jackson on his notification as to the necessity, to attack and punish the enemy if prudent. Answer.

S. D. LEE,

Major-General.

OKOLONA, August 27, 1863.

Brigadier-General CHALMERS.

General Jackson reports the enemy has retired from Yazoo.

S. D. LEE,

Major-General.
