WYTHEVILLE, April 21, 1864.

Major STRINGFELLOW:

Your dispatch received. Telegraph General Echold and McCausland of reported preparation of enemy. They must be ready to move any moment. I will return in the morning.

J. C. BRECKINRIDGE.
