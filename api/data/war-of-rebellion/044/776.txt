
GENERAL ORDERS(Numbers 87.), ADJT. AND INSPECTOR GENERAL'S OFFICE, Richmond, Va., December 10, 1864. I.

The following Roll of Honor is published in accordance with Paragraph I, General Orders, Numbers 131, 1863. It will be read to every regiment in the service at the first dress-parade after its receipt.
