HEADQUARTERS DEPARTMENT OF THE MISSOURI,Saint louis, April 30, 1865- 7 p. m.

Brigadier- General CONNOR,

Denver:

Captain Murfey, commissary of subsistence at Fort Leavenworth, says he has forwarded all supplies asked for by staff officer on plains; that large amounts are now going forward on the estimates for one year's supplies from July next.

G. M. DODGE,

Major- General.
