ADAMS' RUN, July 30, 1864.

GENERAL ORDERS, HDQRS. MILITARY DIST. OF FLORIDA, Numbers 34. Lake City, July 31, 1864.

I. Until further orders the headquarters of this district are established at Lake City.

II. The following officers compose the staff of the brigadier-general commanding:

Captain William G. Barth, assistant adjutant-general.

Captain S. A. Moreno, assistant adjutant-general.

Lieutenant Colonel W. K. Beard, assistant inspector-general.

Major H. Goldthwaite, assistant inspector-general.

Captain W. B. Hundley, assistant inspector-general.

Colonel J. W. Robertson, chief engineer.

Major H. R. Teasdale, chief quartermaster.

Major A. M. Bryan, quartermaster.

Major A. B. Noyes, chief of subsistence.

Major W. C. Sibley, commissary of subsistence.

First Lieutenant J. G. Whitsitt, assistant commissary of subsistence.

Captain T. E. Buckman, chief of ordnance.

Second T. E. Barrett, ordnance officer.

Surg. C. B. Gamble, chief surgeon.

First Lieutenant A. M. Jackson, aide-de-camp.

They will be obeyed and respected accordingly.

By command of Brigadier General K. Jackson:

WM. G. BARTH,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
