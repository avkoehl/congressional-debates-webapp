HDQRS. THIRD DIVISION, FORUTEENTH ARMY CORPS,Near Fort Bunker Hill, D. C., June 9, 1865.

Colonel J. W. BISHOP,

Commanding First Brigade:

COLONEL: General Baird didrects me to inform you that he expects to be absent for a few days from the division, and will rejoin it on the way to Louisville. General Gleason will assume command of the division this evening.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

JOHN W. ACHESON,

Captain and Assistant Adjutant-General.
