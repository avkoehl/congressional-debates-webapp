HEADQUARTERS LEFT WING, ARMY OF Georgia,December 12, 1864 - 10 p. m.

Major General J. C. DAVIS,

Commanding Fourteenth Corps:

The following dispatch* has just been received from General Sherman, and the major-General commanding desires you to carry out the instructions so far as they relate to your portion of the line.

Very respectfully, your obedient servant,

H. C. RODGERS,

Assistant Adjutant-General.
