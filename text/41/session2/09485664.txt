COMMITTEE TO WAIT ON THE PRESIDENT.
Mr. DAWES. Mr. Speaker, the committee
appointed to wait on the President have dis-
charged that duty, and were instructed by him
to report that he has no further communicant
tion to make to Congress.
The SPEAKER. By concurrent resolution
of the two Houses, Friday, July 15, at five
o'clock p. m., is assigned as the time for the
adjournment; and that hour having arrived, I
now declare the House of Representatives of
the Forty-First Congress for its second ses-
sion adjourned without day.
