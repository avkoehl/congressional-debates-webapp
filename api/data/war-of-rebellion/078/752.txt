HEADQUARTERS FORREST'S CAVALRY,Okolona, August 4, 1864.

Brigadier General JAMES R. CHALMERS,

Commanding First DIVISION:

GENERAL: I am instructed by the major-general commanding to say that you will impress all the negroes on the north side of Tallahatchie River to the number of 500, with axes, and such implements as they can get, to make fortifications at Grenada, Graysport, and Abbeville, and such other places as you may think necessary on the Tallahatchie River. You will blockade all fords on the Tallahatchie except those that you may think proper to fortify.

I am, general, very respectfully, your obedient servant,

CHAS. W. ANDERSON,

Acting Assistant Adjutant-General.
