*Caliber not given on Captain Stefens' official copy.

a Four guns near Alexandria, La., to be accounted for by Major G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

b Four guns near Hempstead, Tex., to be accounted for by Major G. A. Magruder, chief of artillery, District of Western Texas.

c Four guns near Nacogdoches, Tex., to be accounted for by Major General G. A. Magruder, chief of artillery, District of Western Texas.

d Four guns with Major-General Shelby's command in Northern Texas; exact location not known; to be accounted for by Captain Collins.

e No guns; serving heavy battery at Grand Ecore, La.

f Four guns near Camden, to be accounted for by Major G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

g No guns with Reserve Battalion, Marshall, Tex.

h Six guns near Tyler, Tex., to be accounted for by Major G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

i Four guns near Tyler, Ark., to be accounted for by Ma. G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

j Four guns near Natchitoches, La., to be accounted for by Major G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

k Four guns, Shreveport Arsenal, La., to be accounted for by Major G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

l Two guns near Shreveport, La., to be accounted for by Major G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

m Four guns near Collinsburg, Bossier Parish, to be accounted for by Major G. O. Watts, chief of artillery, District of Western Louisiana and Arkansas.

n Six guns near Doaksville, C. N., to be accounted for by Captain Howell, reporting to Brigadier General D. H. Cooper.

o Four guns near Jonesville, Tex., (25 miles from Shreveport), to be accounted for Major W. B. Krumbhaar.

p Four guns, District of Texas, exact location unknown, to be accounted for by Major General G. A. Magruder, chief of artillery, District of Texas.
