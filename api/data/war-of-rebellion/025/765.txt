RICHARD, November 28, 1862.

Lieutenant-General HOLMES,

Little Rock, via Vicksburg:

General Pemberton on 27th reports a considerable force from Helena is moving down the river in boats, and an enemy advancing in his front. Other information is that an attack on Vicksburg is threatened.*

---------------

*This paragraph telegraphed also to General Bragg at Tullahoma.
