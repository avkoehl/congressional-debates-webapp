WAR DEPARTMENT,

Washington, August 8, 1862.

Major-General CURTIS, Cairo, Ill.:

It is decided that no land expedition will be moved on Vicksburg at present. The fortification of Helena is approved. You can obtain guns at Bird's Point, Columbus, or Fort Pillow. Use the negroes for that purpose, so far as you can advantageously.

H. W. HALLECK,

General-in-Chief.

SAINT LOUIS, MO., August 8, 1862.

General TOTTEN, Jefferson City, Mo.:

Major Montgomery routed a part of Coffee's force in northern part of Dade County yesterday. He is hot after the remainder, and will continue as long as they are in the State.

Inform your troops, so that they can co-operate.

J. M. SCHOFIELD,

Brigadier-General.
