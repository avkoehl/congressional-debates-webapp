#Colonel Landram commanding vice, Brigadier General S. G. Burbridge, relieved by Special Orders, Numbers 83, Headquarters Nineteenth Army Corps United States Forces, December 5, and granted leave of absence by Special Orders, Numbers 307, Department of the Gulf, December 9. (Special Orders, Nos. 309, 314, Department of the Gulf, of those dates) and reported December 31,both as en route to and at Decrow's Point, Tex.
