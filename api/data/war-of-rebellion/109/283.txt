GEENAL: I am in receipt of your letter iforming me that you have received telegraphic instructions fromteh honorable Secretary of War to proceed to Columbus, ohio, to organize paroled prisoners for service aginst the Indians in the Norhwest, an asking me to appoint your successor in the command of the forces int the vicnity of Covington and Newport, Ky.
