
GENERAL ORDERS, ADJT. AND INSPECTOR GENERAL'S OFFICE,

Numbers 64. Richmond, Va., August 10, 1864.

I. The following Roll of Honor is published in accordance with Paragraph I, General Orders, Numbers 131, 1863. It will be read to every regiment in the service at the first dress-parade after its receipt.

* * * * * * *

BATTLE OF WILLIAMSBURG.

Alabama.

Eighth Regiment of Infantry:

Private William H. Duke,* Co. A.

Private J. R. Philips, Co. C.

Corpl. William H. Powell,* Co. D.

Private James Ganavan, Co. I.

No selections from other companies.
